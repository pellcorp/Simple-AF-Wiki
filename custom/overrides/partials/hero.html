Simple AF Project

This project is designed to assist with the installation of one of six supported ABL probes on Creality K1 Series printers: the Cartographer3D Survey-Touch, the Beacon, the BTT Eddy, the Biqu Microprobe, the BLTouch/CRTouch, and the Voron Klicky. For the Creality K1 Max, this project also facilitates the replacement of the LiDAR module. The guide provides detailed instructions for installing the Simple AF firmware and your selected probe, replacing Creality's proprietary firmware. This involves swapping Creality's G-code and their modified version of Klipper with an official Klipper fork, offering enhanced functionality and performance.
